About Me

My name is Mikhail, and I’m an iOS developer with six years experience. I began learning programming through online courses on Udemy, but most of my growth came from hands-on practice and working on real-world tasks.

I enjoy the full development cycle — from the initial idea to a production-ready app. I prefer implementing all solutions independently, from architecture to custom animations. I have a special passion for crafting unique user interfaces that feel intuitive, smooth, and visually appealing.
