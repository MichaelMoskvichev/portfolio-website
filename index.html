About Me

My name is Mikhail, and I’m an iOS developer with nearly four years of experience. I began learning programming through online courses on Udemy, but most of my growth came from hands-on practice and working on real-world tasks.

I enjoy the full development cycle — from the initial idea to a production-ready app. I prefer implementing all solutions independently, from architecture to custom animations. I have a special passion for crafting unique user interfaces that feel intuitive, smooth, and visually appealing.

I'm detail-oriented and value clean, readable code. I thrive both in team environments and in leadership roles. My colleagues often describe me as calm, respectful, and focused on delivering high-quality results.

Outside of development, I enjoy working out, going for walks, playing video games, and building model ships and cars — a hobby that teaches patience and precision.
